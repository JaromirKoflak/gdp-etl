8 GDP Deflator

A short report of GDP deflators used for estimating nominal GDP for the year 2024.

Deflator_DSIB shows GDP deflators sourced from UNCTAD DSIB, which are used for estimating the nominal GDP for 2024.
Deflator_UNSD is computed separately using data from this ETL script.
